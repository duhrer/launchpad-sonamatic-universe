"Fretboard" Demo

A demonstration of the launchpad tuned as if it were a guitar fretboard. The leftmost pads are the "open" tuning for the string. The bottom six rows correspond to the strings on a guitar in standard tuning. The top two rows add "high A" and "high D" string. The top left arrow controls act as a "capo", so that you can play higher or lower. The up and down arrows change the tuning by an octave, the left and right arrows change the tuning a step at a time.
