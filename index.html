Welcome to the Launchpad Sonamatic Universe

This project presents a range of experiences inspired by and designed for the Novation Launchpad. You can try most of them with just a compatible browser (at time of writing, only Chrome and derivatives, such as Edge, Opera, and Brave). Many of these experiences do not produce sound on their own, but require you to connect an external MIDI device or software solution.

For the best experience, a Novation Launchpad is recommended. A compatible Launchpad controller can operate most of these experiences. In most cases, the lights of the Launchpad also form part of the user interface.

These experiences are well tested on the original Launchpad Pro (sometimes referred to as the "Mark Two") as well as the Launchpad Pro 3. There is also limited support for the original Launchpad (with the Ableton badge in the corner). If you have another model of Launchpad and would like to help add support, please create an issue to talk through the details.

Choose a Journey

"Onscreen" UI

A demonstration of an "onscreen" version of the Launchpad that generates and responds to (most of) the same MIDI messages. Requires an external MIDI device to produce sound.

"Aftertouch"

Visualise aftertouch by lighting the pads surrounding played notes. Requires an external MIDI device to produce sound.

"Fretboard"

An alternate tuning for the launchpad that emulates a guitar fretboard. Can be used with multiple units to create a single large virtual fretboard. Requires an external MIDI device to produce sound.

"Polar Vortex"

A playable vortex with (configurable) gravity and rotation. Pads are held to input energy, which results in swirls of lights and notes. Does not require an external MIDI device to produce sound.
